Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is SkillMitra?

SkillMitra is a platform connecting skilled individuals with safe and fair job opportunities in their local areas.

How do I sign up as a worker?

Click the "Sign Up as a Worker" button above and fill in your details to start connecting with job opportunities.

Is SkillMitra free to use?

Yes, workers can sign up and use SkillMitra for free. Employers may have subscription plans based on their hiring needs.

Are workers verified?

Yes, SkillMitra conducts a thorough verification process to ensure workers are skilled and reliable.

How does SkillMitra ensure fair wages?

We provide tools and guidance to ensure workers are compensated fairly for their skills and services.
